Exercise 10.

(Source: [1], Chapter 5, Exercise 5.3.2)

Let \(T \) be a linear transformation induced by the matrix \(A = \begin{bmatrix} 3 \amp 1 \\ -1 \amp 2 \end{bmatrix} \) and \(S \) a linear transformation induced by \(B = \begin{bmatrix} 0 \amp -2 \\ 4 \amp 2 \end{bmatrix}. \) Find the matrix of \(S \circ T \) and find \((S \circ T )(\mathbf{x}) \) for \(\mathbf{x} = \begin{bmatrix} 2 \\ -1 \end{bmatrix}. \)
