Which of the following sets are subspace of \(\mathbb{R}^3? \) Explain.
  1. \(\displaystyle V_1 = \left\{u = \begin{bmatrix} u_1 \\ u_2 \\ u_3 \end{bmatrix} \ \Bigg| \ |u_1| \le 4 \right\}. \)
  2. \(\displaystyle V_2 = \left\{u = \begin{bmatrix} u_1 \\ u_2 \\ u_3 \end{bmatrix} \ \Bigg| \ u_i \ge 0 \ \text{for each } i = 1,2,3 \right\}. \)
  3. \(\displaystyle V_3 = \left\{u = \begin{bmatrix} u_1 \\ u_2 \\ u_3 \end{bmatrix} \ \Bigg| \ u_3 + u_1 = 2u_2 \right\}. \)
  4. \(\displaystyle V_4 = \left\{u = \begin{bmatrix} u_1 \\ u_2 \\ u_3 \end{bmatrix} \ \Bigg| \ u_3 \ge u_1 \right\}. \)
  5. \(\displaystyle V_5 = \left\{u = \begin{bmatrix} u_1 \\ u_2 \\ u_3 \end{bmatrix} \ \Bigg| \ u_3 = u_1 =0 \right\}. \)
