Suppose that \(\vec{v_1}, \ldots, \vec{v_k}\) are vectors in \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{.}\) The vectors \(\vec{v_1}, \ldots, \vec{v_k}\) are linearly dependent if and only if there is some \(i\text{,}\) \(1 \leq i \leq k\text{,}\) such that \(\vec{v_i}\) is in \(\span(\vec{v_1}, \ldots, \vec{v_{i-1}}, \vec{v_{i+1}}, \ldots, \vec{v_k})\text{.}\)
