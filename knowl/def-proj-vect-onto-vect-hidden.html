Definition: If \(\mbf{v}\) and \(\mbf{w}\) are two vectors in \(\mbb{R}^n\) and \(\mbf{v}\neq \mbf{0}\text{,}\) then the projection of \(\mbf{w}\) onto \(\mbf{v}\) is the vector \(\operatorname{proj}_{\mathbf{v}}(\mathbf{w})\) defined by
\begin{equation*} \operatorname{proj}_{\mathbf{v}}(\mathbf{w}) = \left( \frac{\mbf{v}\cdot \mbf{w}}{\mbf{v}\cdot \mbf{v}} \right) \mbf{v} . \end{equation*}
