Exercise 5.

(Source: [1], Chapter 4, Example 4.125)

Consider the set of vectors given by
\begin{equation*} \{ \mathbf{u_1}, \mathbf{u_2}\}= \left\{ \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}, \begin{bmatrix}-1 \\ 1 \end{bmatrix}\right\}. \end{equation*}
Show that it is an othogonal set of vectors but not an orthonormal one. Find the corresponding orthonormal set.
