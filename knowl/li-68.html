Item 1.2.2.b.
\begin{align*} \norm{\mbf{v}}^2 \amp = \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ 2\end{bmatrix} \cdot \begin{bmatrix} 1 \\ -1 \\ 2\end{bmatrix} \\ \amp = 1\cdot 1+(-1)\cdot (-1)+2\cdot 2 = 1+1+4 = 6 . \end{align*}

Therefore, \(\norm{\mbf{v}}=\sqrt{\mbf{v}\cdot\mbf{v}} =\sqrt{6}\text{.}\)
