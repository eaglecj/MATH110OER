Item 1.2.2.d.
\begin{align*} \norm{\mbf{v}}^2 \amp = \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 0 \\ 2\end{bmatrix} \cdot \begin{bmatrix} -1 \\ 0 \\ 2\end{bmatrix} \\ \amp = (-1)\cdot (-1) + 0\cdot 0 + 2\cdot 2 = 1+0+4 = 5 . \end{align*}

Therefore, \(\norm{\mbf{v}}=\sqrt{\mbf{v}\cdot\mbf{v}} =\sqrt{5}\text{.}\)
