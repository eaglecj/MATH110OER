Let \(\vec{v_1}, \ldots, \vec{v_k}\) be vectors in \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{,}\) and let \(\vec{w}\) be a vector in \(\mathbb{R}^n\) as well. Then \(\vec{w}\) is in \(\span(\vec{v_1}, \ldots, \vec{v_k})\) if and only if the linear system represented by \([\vec{v_1} \cdots \vec{v_k} | \vec{w}]\) is consistent.
