Suppose that \(\vec{v}\text{,}\) \(\vec{w}\text{,}\) and \(\vec{z}\) are vectors in \(\mathbb{R}^n\text{,}\) and \(c\) is a scalar. Then:

  1. \(\displaystyle \vec{v} \cdot \vec{w} = \vec{w} \cdot \vec{v} \)
  2. \(\displaystyle \vec{v} \cdot (\vec{w} + \vec{z}) = \vec{v} \cdot \vec{w} + \vec{v} \cdot \vec{z} \)
  3. \(\displaystyle (c\vec{v}) \cdot \vec{w} = c(\vec{v} \cdot \vec{w}) \)
