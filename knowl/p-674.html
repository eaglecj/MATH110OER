It is important to note that \(\span(\vec{v_1}, \ldots, \vec{v_k})\) is a collection of vectors, and is not a vector. An expression like \(\span(\vec{v_1}, \ldots, \vec{v_k}) = a_1\vec{v_1}+\cdots+a_k\vec{v_k}\) is meaningless, because the left side is a collection of vectors and the right side is a single vector.
